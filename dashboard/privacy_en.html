Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 24, 2026

K-Vant ("Company") protects the personal information of its users and handles complaints related to it promptly and smoothly in accordance with the Information and Communications Network Act and the Personal Information Protection Act.

1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

The Company processes the minimum required personal information for the following purposes. Collected personal information will not be used for any purpose other than those listed below.

  • 1. Member Registration and Management: Confirmation of intent to join, identification/authentication for providing member services, maintenance/management of member qualification, prevention of unauthorized use of services, sending customized reports, email verification (OTP).
  • 2. Provision of Goods or Services: Summarized global shopping trend data transmission, payment, settlement of fees, provision of customized B2B services.
  • 3. Marketing and Advertising: Development of new services and provision of customized services, provision of opportunities to participate in events (upon consent).

2. Items of Personal Information Collected

The Company collects and processes the following personal information items to provide B2B services and free trial benefits:

  • Mandatory items: Email address, password, name, affiliation (company/brand name), primary platforms, primary categories.
  • Optional items: Email for receiving event notifications.
  • Automatically collected items: IP address, cookies, service usage records, access logs, payment records.

3. Period of Processing and Retention of Personal Information

The Company processes and retains personal information within the personal information retention/use period required by law or the period agreed upon when collecting personal information from the user.

  • Member Registration and Management: Until withdrawal from membership. However, if there are outstanding obligations, until the resolution of such obligations.
  • Financial/Payment Information: Up to 5 years in accordance with relevant laws.

4. Provision and Entrustment of Personal Information to Third Parties

The Company processes users' personal information only within the range specified in Section 1 and does not exceed the initial range or provide it to third parties without the user's prior consent. However, for the smooth provision of services (payment, email sending, etc.), we entrust personal information processing tasks as follows:

  • Authentication and Data Storage: Supabase (registration authentication, cloud database).
  • Email Delivery: SMTP hosting providers and Supabase Auth mail servers.
  • Payment Processing: Portone and Stripe (for paid plan payments).

5. Rights of Users and How to Exercise Them

Users can inquire or modify their registered personal information at any time and can request termination of membership. If you contact the personal information manager by mail or email, we will take action without delay.
